Captain Halloway adjusted his brass goggles as the Stellar Gear dipped into the ionized clouds of the Clockwork Nebula. Here, the vacuum of space didn't scream; it ticked.

Massive, rusted cogs the size of moons rotated in a slow, celestial dance, driven by gravitational forces no scientist in the Core Worlds could explain.

"Steam pressure at eighty percent," the navigator called out, his voice muffled by a leather mask. They weren't just looking for scrap; they were looking for the Origin Key.
